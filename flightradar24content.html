Flightradar24

A hobby that got out of hand, that's how Flightradar24 started in 2006.

By now they provide a helping hand to every tech-savvy traveller.

Whether you want to check your own, your spouse's or friend's flight, which airlines operate at which airport, or want to see whether that's an A380 which flew over your head, Flightradar24 is the platform!

Who's the boss (and what did he say)?

There is no bragging about the leadership team on their website, but the CEO Fredrik Lindahl can be found on LinkedIn.

He's been running the show since 2012 and is together with Mikael Robertsson the owner of the business.
